Our Story
Ace Rithm is an emerging company founded by Ace, a self-taught tonsorial artist with over twenty years of experience dedicated to his craft. Here at Ace Rithm, we specialize in all grades of hair along with a bevy of various styles and techniques. We stay up to date with the latest industry trends and As a company, we pride ourselves on excellence and ensure you're getting top-notch service. We take the time to perfect your cut and make sure it's exactly how you like it. And as we say here, "We ain't satisfied until you satisfied."
